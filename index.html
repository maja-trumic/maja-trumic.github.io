About

I am a Research Associate at the ETF Robotics, University of Belgrade. I finished bilateral Ph.D. studies in 2021 in the field of robotics and automation at the University of Palermo, Italy, and the University of Belgrade, Serbia. My research is dedicated to the modeling and control of soft-bodied robots, as well as estimating the stiffness in robot joints, in order to achieve safe human-robot interaction and effective performance of dynamic tasks.
